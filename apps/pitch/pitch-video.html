The economic problem

AI agents still can’t pay
each other per action.

The unit of value is one API call, one artifact unlock, one machine action. Subscriptions are too coarse, gas is too expensive, and off-chain ledgers kill proof.

×Subscriptions fail when every action needs its own price.
×Traditional gas costs more than a 0.001 USDC transaction is worth.
×Off-chain settlement removes the independent onchain proof judges need to see.
The answer

Agents pay agents.
Per call. Sub-cent. Onchain.

TTM Agent Market prices each artifact in USDC, routes payment to Circle-controlled wallets, verifies settlement on Arc Testnet, and unlocks the artifact only after proof lands.
